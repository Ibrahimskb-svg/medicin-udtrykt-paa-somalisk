Atorvastatin – kolestarool

Kiniin afka ah

Akhris fudud oo kooban
Kolestaroolka xun marar badan lama dareemo, laakiin si aamusnaan ah ayuu xididdada dhiigga ugu ururi karaa dufan. Atorvastatin waxay ka caawisaa jirka inuu yareeyo kolestaroolkaas, si waddada dhiiggu u ahaato mid ka sii nadiifsan oo khatartu u yaraato.
Talo gaar ah oo Ibraahim ka timid

Waxaan dadka ugu sharxaa sidan: haddii dufan yar-yar uu si tartiib ah ugu dhego gudaha xididdada dhiigga, marinku wuu cidhiidhi noqon karaa sida tuubo wasakh ku uruurtay. Atorvastatin ma aha daawo aad isla markiiba dareemayso, balse waa daawo si deggan u ilaalisa wadnaha iyo maskaxda mustaqbalka.
